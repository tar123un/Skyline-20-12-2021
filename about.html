About

Inspiring through change.

Our organization is a global manufacturer and exporter of  Bed linen (Top of Bed). With advanced facilities and modern machinery, we provide our customers with the satisfaction of a timely delivery as always.
We cater to various clients and our customer base reaches around the globe and  extends to more than 4 continents.

We combine two principles innovative design and good quality which helps us provide our customers with quality that is unrivaled in the industry. We are surrounded with options but choosing Skyline comes with an understanding that you are working with a company whose products are made from ethical supply chains and manufactured with a workforce whose well being is prioritized inside and outside the workplace.

The Skyline Mantra … ‘Quality above all’ We value quality above all else and serving our customer to their utmost satisfaction is our core belief. Within every stage of the production process, from the selection of yarn to the packaging of the product, well experienced quality inspectors monitor and supervise every product with the best attention to detail. Every employee from the designer to the on-floor employee is taught the value of delivering quality goods which drives the workforce to strive for excellence, driving sales and improving customer satisfaction.

The Skyline Family, at Skyline it is not only our core belief to keep our customers satisfied but also our workforce satisfied. The well-being of our employees is a priority inside and outside of the workplace. At Skyline we believe that employees are family and we treat them as such.

Employee Benefit Programs

Our company is 'Fair Trade' certified, where social, economic and environmental aspects of production are certified and a portion of the profits from each product sold is provided to our employees in additions to their wages.

Employee Benefit Programs

Our company provides basic Supplies for day to day requirements  such as pressure cookers for better quality of food at home to all employees, free sanitary napkins provided to all female employees.
We also offer subsidiaries such as school Fee’s and financial aid provided for the children of our employees for the betterment of their future through education.



Employee Benefit Programs

We offer vocational training to employees for the betterment of their skills. 


Social Programs

We organize programs such as tree plantation drives for the betterment of the environment, donation of groceries to orphanages.
Medical Camps such as check-ups and free vaccination camps are set up in in regular intervals for the betterment of the overall well being of our workers.
Cultural activities during festivals are conducted to promote
participation and team work.
